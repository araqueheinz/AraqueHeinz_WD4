Meet Our Head Chef

Daniel Garcia

At the tender age of 15 I had my first introduction to a professional kitchen. I entered an international culinary school where I achieved my first cuisine certification.

However I thought that my education wasn’t complete, so I decided to take it to the highest possible level and enrolled at Le Cordon Bleu School in London, where I was awarded a “Diplome de Cuisine”.

Some years later, I founded L’Orchidée Perdue and since then it has been my aim to create unforgettable and indulgent fantasies for everyone!

ABOUT US

L’Orchidée Perdue

Chef Daniel Garcia and Elias Dayub discovered a common passion for fine desserts, French and Latin Cuisine. This took them on a fantastic journey to create an incredible range of handmade patisseries, dishes and more.

Combining over 12 years of experience and a lifetime of passion, L’Orchidée Perdue was created.

At L’orchidée Perdue we are passionate about delivering quality over the highest expectation. We offer you an amazing range of our highly fashionable, yet classic French-Latin Cuisine including pastries and more.
